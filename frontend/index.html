Calliope Help & Guide

Welcome to Calliope! Your advanced vocabulary learning platform. Here's how to make the most of each feature:

Word of the Day

Start each day with a new sophisticated vocabulary word. Each word includes:

  • Definition: Clear, precise meaning
  • Example: Contextual usage in a sentence
  • Rarity: Common Advanced (Notty), Less Common (Luke), or Rare/Archaic (Alex)
  • Sentiment: Emotional tone (Positive, Negative, Neutral, Formal)

Add Word

Expand your vocabulary database with AI assistance:

  • Enter any word and get automatic definitions and examples
  • Review and edit AI-generated content before saving
  • Get spelling suggestions for misspelled words
  • Accept, edit, or reject words before adding to your database

Database

Browse and manage your entire vocabulary collection:

  • Search: Find words by name or definition with autocomplete
  • Filter: Sort by part of speech, rarity, or sentiment
  • Manage: View, edit, or delete words (right-click for quick actions)
  • Navigate: Use pagination controls to browse large collections

Thesaurus

Discover word relationships within your vocabulary:

  • Enter any word to find synonyms from your personal database
  • See full word information for each synonym
  • Understand connections between words you've learned

Prediction

Get AI-powered word suggestions for sentence completion:

  • Enter a sentence with an underscore (_) where you want suggestions
  • Filter suggestions by sentiment or part of speech
  • Click any suggestion to insert it into your sentence
  • Learn contextual word usage and practice vocabulary

Flashcards

Practice vocabulary through interactive flashcards:

  • Filters: Customize by part of speech, rarity, sentiment, or count
  • Navigation: Use arrows, click to flip, or keyboard shortcuts
  • Shortcuts: ← Previous | → Next | ↑/Space Flip | S Shuffle
  • Study: Click cards to reveal definitions and examples

Analyzer

Enhance your writing with vocabulary upgrades:

  • Paste any text to identify enhancement opportunities
  • Click highlighted words to see sophisticated alternatives
  • Get 2-5 suggestions per word from your vocabulary database
  • Replace words and copy the enhanced text

Statistics

Track your vocabulary learning progress:

  • Monitor total word count and daily additions
  • View learning streaks and growth patterns
  • Analyze distribution by part of speech, rarity, and sentiment
